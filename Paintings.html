Beauty lies in our perception. We need to realize, recognize, invent and create the beauty. You can think about the beauty of what we people make, with our mind, with our hands, you can think about the beauty of a relationship between two people, but the awareness of the beauty of nature is also a human achievement. I believe it was Oscar Wilde who said that no one ever saw the fog in Londen until Turner painted it.

 
(György Konräd in; Wim Kayzer, Het boek van de schoonheid en van de troost, p 198-199)
